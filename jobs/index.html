About Tile38

Tile38 is a location intelligence company. We build high-performance geospatial and real-time geofencing technology that makes it super easy for companies to connect to and manage the locations of their fleet, mobile apps, and IoT devices.

We maintain the Tile38 open source database and we’re actively building some amazing new software for SaaS and enterprise users.

The Tile38 project was started by Josh Baker in 2015, the v1.0 open source software was released in 2016, and the company was officially founded in 2017. Josh has a passion for well-crafted software and beautiful interfaces. He’s been entrepreneuring for 15 years and his previous companies (Shotzoom/Brushfire) have built a series of world-class products. Accolades: Apple’s Top 5 Grossing, Apple’s Design of the Year Award, Starbucks “App of the Week,” and a Microsoft Case Study.
